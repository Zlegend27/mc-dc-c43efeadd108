Step 1 · Every check-in

📋 Paste today's Shopify report

Paste the combined export — store, today's sales, LY same-weekday sales, and (once your reports include it) LY average order. Matches every store by name and fills everything in below in one shot.

Step 2 · As needed

✏️ Opener / Closer templates

Wording and the sales-floor rule for each part of the week. Every text also gets an automatic stat drop (LY total, LY average order, $/hr) appended below your message. LY numbers themselves come from Step 1's paste, or can be tweaked right on each store's card in Send.

Use {name}, {amount}, and {day} (fills in the real day, e.g. "Friday" or "Sunday" — handy since this bucket covers more than one day). {amount} is today's live goal (LY vs. the floor above, minus today's sales). The stat drop is added automatically, using 10hrs for Openers and 5hrs for Closers.
Step 3 · 1pm & 5pm checks

📉 Low Sales template

Sent only to stores that are behind on actual sales today — not tied to the opener/closer goal above.

Use {name}, {amount}, and {day} — {amount} is that store's real LY gap (no floor, no stat drop).

Edit a phone number, add a new hire, or add a brand-new store — without re-pasting the whole contact sheet.
