Step 1 · Every check-in

📋 Paste today's Shopify report

Paste the combined export — store, today's sales, LY same-weekday sales, and (once your reports include it) LY average order. Matches every store by name and fills everything in below in one shot.

Step 2 · As needed

✏️ Opener / Closer templates

Wording and the minimum daily goal for each part of the week. Every text also gets an automatic numbers block (LY total, LY average order, $/hr) appended below your message. LY numbers themselves come from Step 1's paste, or can be tweaked right on each store's card in Send.

Use {name}, {amount}, and {day} (fills in the real day, e.g. "Friday" or "Sunday" — handy since these days share one message). {amount} is today's live goal (last year's number, or the minimum above if that's higher, minus what's sold today). The numbers block is added automatically, and paces itself against the real hours left until that store closes today (set per store in Contacts).
Step 3 · 1pm & 5pm checks

📉 Low Sales template

Sent only to stores that are behind on actual sales today — not tied to the opener/closer goal above.

Use {name}, {amount}, and {day} — {amount} is how far behind that store is today vs. last year (no minimum applied, no numbers block added).
